Chapter 11

  1. The first is called the AND gate which is a circuit that produces an output signal only when signals are received simultaneously through all input connections. Then there’s an OR gate which is a circuit that gives an output signal if there is a signal on any of its inputs. The third and the NOR gate. A circuit that produces an output signal only when there are no signals on any of the input connections. Lastly is the NAND gate. A circuit that produces an output signal until there are signals on all of its inputs.
